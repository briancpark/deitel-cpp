Examples

Once you have installed all the software, you are ready to begin working through the exercises in the book.

Click to load the examples window.

This will bring you to a window that shows the examples directories of the CD. They are arranged by chapter number and contain the example code for each particular chapter. You can copy them to your hard drive and modify them as you work your way through the book.

You may also click directly on the Examples button on the startup screen, or you may use the Windows Explorer to copy the examples directly from the CD to your hard drive.
